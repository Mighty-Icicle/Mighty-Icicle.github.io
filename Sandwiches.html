It is currently 495øC and my sandwich is melting
I did a few more tests and found that the universal boiling point for a cheese sandwich is 486øC
Curiously however it seems that a humble pickle sandwich has a much lower boiling point at 393øC

Thanks to my total lack of social life and an alarming amount of free time I managed to create a list of all of the melting points for sandwiches;

Peanut butter and jam sandwich: 370øC
Marmite sandwich: 385øC
Pickle sandwich: 393øC
BLT sandwich: 460øC
Cheese sandwich: 486øC
Bacon sandwich: 515øC
Ham sandwich: 520øC
Club sandwich: 633øC
Knuckle sandwich: 854øC
